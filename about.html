Our Story

We are three best friends from the South Bay of Los Angeles, California. Our goal is to deliver more sustainable goods without compromising aesthetics. We are deeply rooted to our community, and we are committed to donating a percentage of profits to those in need. This not only applies to our commounity but also injustices we do not stand for.

All of our custom bouquets, special orders, and event furnishings are made to order. We want to promote a collaborative atmosphere and will ask you a few additional questions because we are inspired by you. We are currently in the works of creating virtual workshops that are complementary with a purchase of dried floral bundles.
